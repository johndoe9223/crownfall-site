Crownfall - Rules of Play

Game Overview

Players: 2–4
Deck: Standard 52-card deck (no Jokers)
Theme: Rival nobles scheming for power in a crumbling kingdom.
Objective: Be the first to earn 10 points by capturing powerful card stacks — or eliminate your rivals by draining their hands.

Setup

  • Remove Jokers from the deck.
  • Shuffle the deck thoroughly.
  • Deal 5 cards to each player.
  • Place the rest face down as the Kingdom Deck (draw pile).
  • Flip 1 card face up to form the Battlefield.
  • Each player starts with 0 points.

Turn Actions (Clockwise)

Each player must take one of the following actions:

1. Challenge

  • Play a card face up on the Battlefield.
  • Must be equal or higher than the top card.
  • Increases stack's Crown Power.
  • If empty, play any card to start.

2. Bluff

  • Play a card face down and declare it.
  • Other players can call the bluff.
  • If bluff is false: lose 2 cards; challenger scores stack.
  • If true: challenger loses 2 cards; play continues.

3. Fold

  • Take the Battlefield stack and score its value.
  • Draw 2 cards from the Kingdom Deck.
  • Reset the Battlefield with a new face-up card.

Scoring

Face Values: A = 1, J = 11, Q = 12, K = 13

Total the face value of all numbered cards in the stack when folding and add it to your score.

Victory: First to 10+ points wins. If you run out of cards and can’t draw, you're eliminated.

Strategy Tips

  • Bluff high to intimidate.
  • Play low to bait.
  • Fold at the right time to steal points.
  • Manage your hand carefully — cards are power!
